Founder and Creative Director of MALACHI

Malachi is a collective that exists to empower men and celebrate their accomplishments through bespoke clothing. Born from the belief that the notion of menswear is often static and outdated, men have been blocked unnecessarily from expressing themselves in the world. Where fashion and self-expression have been restricted for men, a revolution is imminent!
